┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ HOW TO USE NEW FEATURES                                                      │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  FOR HR MANAGERS:
  ════════════════

  1. Go to http://localhost:3000/hr
  2. Scroll to "Job Positions" section
  3. Click "Add New Job Position"
  4. Fill in:
     - Job Title
     - Job Description
     - Requirements (add multiple)
     - Application Deadline
  5. Click "Create Job Position"
  6. Job appears in Active Jobs list
  7. Click on accepted applicant → "Send Email"
  8. Fill email subject and message
  9. Click "Send Email"

  FOR APPLICANTS:
  ════════════════

  1. Go to http://localhost:3000/apply
  2. See job details with requirements
  3. Check deadline (must not be passed)
  4. If multiple jobs, select one
  5. Fill application form
  6. Upload resume (PDF or Word)
  7. Click "Submit"
  8. AI evaluates instantly
  9. See results (Accepted/Rejected)
  10. If accepted: "Check your emails for interview details"
